National Service Training Program (NSTP)

Civic welfare training service for developing student citizenship and community involvement

Civic Education Service-Oriented All Levels

Course Description

The National Service Training Program (NSTP) is a civic education and defense preparedness program for Filipino college students. It aims to enhance civic consciousness and defense preparedness in the youth by developing the ethics of service and patriotism.

Course Details

  • Type: Civic Welfare Training Service
  • Focus: Community Service & Citizenship
  • Duration: One Academic Year
  • Components: ROTC, CWTS, LTS

Course Information

NSTP focuses on practical community service and civic responsibility

Program Components

ROTC - Reserve Officers' Training Corps

Military training program providing education and training in preparation for national defense

CWTS - Civic Welfare Training Service

Community service activities contributing to the general welfare and betterment of life

LTS - Literacy Training Service

Training program designed to train students to teach literacy and numeracy skills

Program Objectives

Develop Civic Consciousness

Instill patriotism and nationalism in the youth

Promote Community Involvement

Encourage active participation in community affairs

Enhance Defense Preparedness

Train students for national defense and security

Note

NSTP resources and requirements are typically provided by your institution's NSTP office. Please check with your NSTP coordinator for specific materials and community service requirements for your campus.
