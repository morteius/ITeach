Physics for Computing

Explore the fundamental physics concepts that power modern computing systems

Core Subject 14 Lessons Intermediate Level

Course Description

This course covers the essential physics principles that underlie modern computing systems. From basic thermodynamics to semiconductor physics and sensor technology, you'll gain a deep understanding of how physical phenomena enable digital computing.

Course Details

  • Duration: Full Semester Course
  • Prerequisites: Basic Physics Knowledge
  • Format: Downloadable PDFs & DOCX Files
  • Assessment: Midterm & Final Topics
