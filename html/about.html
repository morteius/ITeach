About ITeach

Empowering IT students with free resources and tools for academic success

Our Story

Welcome to ITeach - a platform created by IT students, for IT students

Our Purpose

ITeach was born from the realization that many IT students struggle to find comprehensive, organized, and free resources for their courses. We're here to bridge that gap.

Student-Driven

Created by current BSIT students who understand the challenges of the curriculum. We know what you need because we need it too.

Future Vision

Starting with first-year courses, we plan to expand to cover the entire IT curriculum and become the go-to resource hub for IT students nationwide.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, free educational resources and tools for Information Technology students. We strive to create an accessible platform where students can easily find study materials, courses, and tools to support their academic journey and enhance their understanding of IT concepts.

Our Vision

To become the leading free educational resource platform for IT students in the Philippines, empowering thousands of students to succeed academically and develop the skills needed for their future careers in technology.

What We Offer

Comprehensive resources designed specifically for IT students

Structured Courses

Organized course materials with downloadable resources, step-by-step guides, and comprehensive coverage of IT subjects.

Video Resources

Curated YouTube tutorials and educational videos that complement course materials and provide visual learning.

Practical Tools

Essential software recommendations, programming tools, and utilities that every IT student needs for their projects.
