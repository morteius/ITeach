Frequently Asked Questions

Can we use this website even if we are not from Bicol University?

Yes, the resources we provide are applicable to students outside our university, especially BSIT students. While there may be some differences, we strive to ensure that the topics you’re looking for related to BSIT are available here.

Can students from other programs use this website?

Unfortunately, we primarily recommend this website for BSIT students or those in fields related to IT, as most topics focus on the BSIT curriculum. However, some lessons cover General Elective courses, which might be useful for students in other programs.

Is this website exclusive to first-year students?

Currently, the lessons are tailored for first-year students, as that’s our current level. However, we plan to expand and include lessons for higher college levels in the future.
