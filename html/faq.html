Common Questions

Browse through our frequently asked questions to find quick answers

Can we use this website even if we are not from Bicol University?

+

Yes, the resources we provide are applicable to students outside our university, especially BSIT students. While there may be some differences, we strive to ensure that the topics you're looking for related to BSIT are available here.

Can students from other programs use this website?

+

Unfortunately, we primarily recommend this website for BSIT students or those in fields related to IT, as most topics focus on the BSIT curriculum. However, some lessons cover General Elective courses, which might be useful for students in other programs.

Is this website exclusive to first-year students?

+

Currently, the lessons are tailored for first-year students, as that's our current level. However, we plan to expand and include lessons for higher college levels in the future.

Are all resources on this website completely free?

+

Yes, all courses, resources, and tools on ITeach are completely free of charge. We believe in making quality IT education accessible to all students without any cost barriers.

How often is new content added to the website?

+

We regularly update our content with new courses, resources, and tools. We aim to add new materials at least once a month, following the academic calendar and student needs.

Can I request specific topics or courses to be added?

+

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from students. You can contact us through our social media channels or email to request specific topics. We prioritize requests based on student demand and relevance to the IT curriculum.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question in our FAQ section, feel free to reach out to us directly. We're here to help!
